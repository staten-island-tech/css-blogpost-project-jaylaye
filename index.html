Sushi

Sushi is a famous japanese dish consisting of rice, seafood, vegatables, and
seaweed. Common ingredients include salmon, eel, avocado, tuna, cucumber, and
crab. The concept of sushi was orginally developed to preserve raw fish in
fermented rice in Southeast Asia.

Udon

Udon is a thick chewy noodle made from wheat flour, water, and salt.
It is usually used in japanese cuisine but many say udon originates from China.
Udon can be served in broth or it could also be stir-fried.

Okonomiyaki

This is a jappanese teppanyaki dish, a style of japanese cuisine that uses a iron griddle
to cooke food. Okonomiyaki is a pancake dish of wheat flour and other ingredients like
cabbage, meat, and seafood. It is often topped with japanese mayonnaise and
katsuobushi which is often used to top takoyaki. katsuobushi also known as bonito
flakes are basically thin fish shavings.
